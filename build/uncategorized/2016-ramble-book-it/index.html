2016 Ramble– Book it!

Posted on January 10, 2016

We have a date for the 2016 Randolph Ramble!

Sunday, October 30th. Save it!

Are we going to make it bigger, badder, and more extravagant than the first annual Randolph Ramble?

Nope. We liked it just the way it was. Low-key. Fun. Rugged trail running. Great friends, new and old. Good food, too!

Save the weekend– we’ll be hosting the Trails in Motion film festival again this year, too.
