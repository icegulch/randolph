Film Festival

On Saturday, October 28st at 7 pm, race partner Run the Alps will present the international film festival, Trails in Motion at the Medallion Opera House in Gorham, New Hampshire.

The Internationally-Acclaimed Trails in Motion film festival features 9 short trail running films, from Patagonia to New England! We are very excited to be able to include these films as part of the weekend.

Purchase tickets online and get information on the venue, right here.

Tickets will also be available the night of the show and at the White Mountain Cafe and Bookstore.
